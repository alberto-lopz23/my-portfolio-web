The house famous

That we see is a famous house, inside and outside. By Alberto lopez.

Moders houses

Jonsh Thomson

This house was built in 1985 by architect Alberto L. Checo. The house was designed so that the family could live in a cozy and comfortable environment. Checo wanted this house to endure over the years as a center of architectural perfection.

Albert L. Checo

He was born in the Dominican Republic in a small town. Since he was a child, with a love for architecture and drawings, he designed countless architectural works, but none like this house, which was very modern for the time. Even now, it is still very modern.
